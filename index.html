Brawlbarrow Hamlet

Brawlbarrow Hamlet is a small human settlement. Designed on the lowest point, the settlement of Brawlbarrow is home to a bunch of brawlers lead by Rixhammer, the best brawler.

This settlement wasn't built by accident, as it has emotional significance, which is of great importance to the people of Brawlbarrow.

The settlement itself looks crude. With its decaying rooftops, worn sandstone walls and dirty roads, Brawlbarrow has a grim atmosphere. The main attraction is the Brawler Hall, which was built 55 years ago and designed by First Brawlers.

Brawlbarrow has a thriving economy, which is mainly supported by thieving, beer brewing and woodcrafting. But their biggest strengths are alchemy and refined cigarette rolling. However, Brawlbarrow lacks people skilled in fishing.

Despite its strengths and weaknesses, Brawlbarrow is most likely headed towards a macabre future under the leadership of Rixhammer. But this remains to be seen.
